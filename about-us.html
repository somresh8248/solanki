Our Team

Dr. Narpat Solanki

Chief Surgeon - Cataract and Refractive Surgeon (MS (OPHTH))

For more than three decades Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital (DSEH) has been serving people from all walks of life with exceptional eye care. From a humble , we have grown to a hospital serving people not only from the city of Bengaluru but across the country and beyond the shores.

Dr. M. Muralidhara Rao

MBBS, MS, DOMS, FRCS (ED) Chief Surgeon & Medical Director, Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital

Specializes in Phaco, Small Incision Cataract Surgery, General Ophthalmology, Glaucoma, & Vitrectomy.

Dr. Sribhargava Natesh

MBBS, DOMS, FMRF, ICO Consultant - Vitreoretinal, Neuro-Ophthalmology & Ocular Oncology Dr. Natesh specializes in Vitreoretinal, Uveitis, Neuro-Ophthalmology, Ocular Oncology, Retinopathy of Prematurity and Trauma.

Dr. S.M.Sundaram

MBBS, DOMS

Dr. Priyank Solanki

MBBS, DOMS, DNB
Cataract & Refractive Surgeon, General Ophthalmology, Assistant Medical Director, Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital

Dr. Sonal G Chhaya

Glaucoma Services, (DOMS, DNB)
Dr. Sonal specializes in Anterior segment surgeries with a special interest in Glaucoma.

Dr. Sarika Ramachandran

MBBS, DNB, FPRS – Consultant Ophthalmologist
Her surgical skills include Manual and Phacoemulsification cataract surgeries, Pterygium surgery, Corneal laser refractive surgery, Intra-ocular phakic lens implantation, Collagen crosslinking, Dacryocystectomy. She has also co-authored publications in national and international journals.
