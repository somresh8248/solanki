Dr. M. Muralidhara Rao

MBBS, MS, DOMS, FRCS (ED)
Chief Surgeon & Medical Director, Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital

Has vast experience of more than 25 yrs in multiple sub-specialties of the subject, as a postgraduate teacher and administrator. He graduated from Government Medical College, Bellary in the year 1981. After an initial residency in Ophthalmology from Kasturba Medical College, Manipal, he completed post-graduation in Ophthalmology from Mysore Medical College in the year 1985. He has extensive work experience in Oman & UK as well.

Specializes in Phaco, Small Incision Cataract Surgery, General Ophthalmology, Glaucoma, & Vitrectomy.

Dr. Priyank Solanki

MBBS, DOMS, DNB
Cataract & Refractive Surgeon, General Ophthalmology, Assistant Medical Director, Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital Chief Surgeon & Medical Director, Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital

Has experience of more than 8 yrs in the field of Cataract & Refractive Surgery. He holds an MBBS degree from MS Ramaiah Medical College, Bangalore, (2008) and graduated from Siddhartha University, Tumkur in the year 2011 and National Board of Examination in 2013. He won the youngest Researcher award in the year 2012 conferred by the Association for Research in Vision and Ophthalmology (ARVO), India Chapter.

Specializes in Cataract & Refractive Department and a keen interest in Training.

Dr. Sribhargava Natesh

MBBS, DOMS, FMRF, ICO
Consultant - Vitreoretinal, Neuro-Ophthalmology & Ocular Oncology

Has over 15 years of experience in the field of Vitreoretinal medicine.

He graduated from Mysore Medical College, completed DOMS from Bangalore Medical College and DNB from National Board, New Delhi. He holds two Fellowships, one in Vitreo Retina from Shankar Nethralaya, Chennai and another in Ocular Oncology from New York.

Dr. Natesh specializes in Vitreoretinal, Uveitis, Neuro-Ophthalmology, Ocular Oncology, Retinopathy of Prematurity and Trauma. He is also committed to the on-going academics and research activities in Ophthalmology.

Dr. Sonal G Chhaya

Glaucoma Services,
(DOMS, DNB)

Has vast experience of more than 30 yrs in multiple sub-specialties of the subject and over a decade in Glaucoma. She graduated from Bombay University in the year 1988, DOMS (Ophthal) from Minto Eye Hospital, Bangalore and DNB from Aravind Eye Care in 1999.

Dr. Sonal specializes in Anterior segment surgeries with a special interest in Glaucoma.

Dr. S.M. Sundaram

MBBS, DOMS
Consultant Ophthalmologist

Has over 20 years of experience in the field of general and surgical Ophthalmology. Has rendered eye care services in renowned corporate and private eye hospitals as a consultant and chief medical officer. He is also a phacoemulsification and SICS trainer for postgraduates and fellow surgeons in Ophthalmology from India and abroad. He renders free surgical eye care services for the poor and needy suffering from cataract blindness in various parts of India under the prestigious Project Drishti organization which is the CSR wing of Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital.

Dr. Sundaram graduated from JSS Medical College, Mysore University in 1994 and completed post-graduation from Mysore Medical College, Mysore University in 1997..

Dr. Sundaram specializes in Phacoemulsification and Small Incision Cataract surgery.

Dr. Sarika Ramachandran

MBBS, DNB, FPRS –
Consultant Ophthalmologist

Dr. Sarika Ramachandran graduated from Sri Devaraj Urs Medical College, Kolar in the year 2005. She completed her DNB (Ophthalmology) from Narayana Nethralaya, Bangalore in the year 2011 and continued with Fellowship in Phacoemulsification and Refractive Surgery in the same institute which was completed in 2013.

In addition to her passion for Cataract surgery and refractive solutions, she holds keen interest in General Ophthalmology. Her surgical skills include Manual and Phacoemulsification cataract surgeries, Pterygium surgery, Corneal laser refractive surgery, Intra-ocular phakic lens implantation, Collagen crosslinking, Dacryocystectomy. She has also co-authored publications in national and international journals.
